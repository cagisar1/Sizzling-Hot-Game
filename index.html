INSTRUCTIONS

In order to play you'll have to first "insert" some cash. In the middle of all the buttons you will find a field named "INSERT CASH". There you will enter the amount of cash you want to play (a number greater or equal to 1).Then you will select the desired bet using the "+" and "-" signs, which you can find in the field named "BET". You can only raise your bet until it's equal to the number of points you have. Also, you can see what's the highest bet you can play on by clicking the "MAX BET" button. When everything is set up you can finally hit the "START" button and enjoy! If you don't want to click on the "START" button for every spin, you can hit the "AUTO" button and it will respin automatically. To see how much each combination of elements/line pays, depending on the chosen bet, find the "INFO" button on the left.When your points get to 0, in order to play you'll have to insert some more cash.The number of lines is fixed.
